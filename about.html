Mr. Joseph Mensah

Joseph Mensah, President of the company First Fashion, is one of the winners of the 9th edition of Fashion's Next Best, the project of fashion scouting for young talents in Ghana's Fashion Industry, created and developed by FashionIsta Ghana. FIG is considered the Ghanaian platform through which the new talented designers have the opportunity to interact with a prestigious international jury of expert, including Mrs Franca Abedi, Director of FashionIsts ghana. Durign the award ceremony she said: "Creativity is free and Joseph Mensah has shown it. His work is special and unique".
